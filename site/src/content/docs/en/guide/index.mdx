hit each task for the first time.

  </LinkCard>

    Enumerate visible cameras and pick one by serial number, user name, IP, or
    MAC address.
  </LinkCard>
  <LinkCard title="Camera configuration" href="/guide/camera-configuration/" icon="seti:config">

    on the open device.
  </LinkCard>
  <LinkCard title="Streaming" href="/guide/streaming/" icon="seti:video">

  </LinkCard>
  <LinkCard title="Image and video writers" href="/guide/image-and-video/" icon="seti:media">
